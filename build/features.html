Features

Home/Features


Backlog Management

Create and prioritize features and enhancements for upcoming sprints

Screen Sharing

Effortlessly record video messages and share with your team

Delivery Tracking

Set goals, track team velocity, predict the release and see if you’re on track or not

Bug Reporting

Quickly identify, prioritize and document relative bugs within the tasks

Task & Workflow Management

Tasks & Subtasks:

Write user stories, break them into small subtasks, set priorities, due date and assign them to team members.


Custom Statuses:

Define unique statuses for your project, like 'in-review' and 'on-hold', to accurately reflect the current state of work.


Bugs Tracking:

Efficiently track and manage bugs directly within tasks and resolve them alongside other task-related activities.

Agile Planning & Tracking

Sprints:

Define sprint period, set goals, allocate tasks to team members, and monitor progress in real time.


Backlog:

Create task lists in the backlog to smartly plan and strategies your user stories, feature requests, and tasks for future sprints or releases.


Progress Board:

Track task movement through various stages on customizable boards to limit work-in-progress, and maximize team’s efficiencies.

Collaboration & Communication

Video Sharing:

Record audio and video messages within tasks to share updates, tasks details and seek feedback from the team


Teams Management:

Organize team members into groups and manage permissions. Add team members in projects, assign tasks and keep track of their velocity.


Files:

Effortlessly create and maintain important project documentation, files, and team guidelines at one place for easy reference and knowledge sharing.

Smart Tools & Integrations

AI:

Use AI to generate tasks from audio and video messages. Refine task detail and generate description with title.


Calendar:

Keep track of important dates, such as sprint start and end dates, task due dates, and team meetings for better project planning and coordination.


Kanban Boards:

Organize tasks into columns representing different stages of progress, such as "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done," for improved progress tracking.

Reports & Analytics

Burn up & Burn Down charts:

Track completed work versus planned, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to stay on course towards project goals.


Project Overview :

Gain a holistic view of project status, including task progress, milestones achieved, and upcoming deadlines, for informed decision-making and reporting purposes.


Sprints Reports:

Analyze sprint metrics like team velocity, burn-up, burn down reports to see what went well and what needs to improve in upcoming sprint.
