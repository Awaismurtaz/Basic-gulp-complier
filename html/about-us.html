About Us


At Scrum Slate, our mission is to develop a tool that is specifically dedicated to Agile Project Management teams. We understand it is harder to achieve the target goals when you don’t have the right tool. With Scrum Slate you get the right features that support sprint planning, backlog management, bug tracking and iterative development cycles to ensure your team stays on track.

Moreover, we understand every team has unique needs and his own set of challenges. That’s why we have made Scrum Slate highly customizable, allowing the team to adapt the tool according to their workspace and preferences. That’s not all, our advanced algorithms analyze user inputs and generate concise, accurate, AI based descriptions, saving valuable time and ensuring clarity throughout the project lifecycle.
